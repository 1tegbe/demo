Welcome

A quiet standard of luxury in the heart of Abuja

Stallion Luxury Suites was built on a simple idea: hospitality should feel personal. Every room is considered down to the last detail — from hand-finished furnishings to artwork sourced locally — so that comfort never comes at the cost of character.

Set within Karu, minutes from the city's business district, the hotel offers the calm of a private residence with the service standard of a five-star house.
